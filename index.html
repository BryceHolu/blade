How it works

  • You start as a square (4 edges).
  • Collect edge parts to add edges up to 26.
  • After 26 edges, edge parts start building Plasma (blades) → increases Attack only.
  • Plasma damage: first hit is 3×, later hits are 1×.
  • Regen parts increase HP regeneration.
